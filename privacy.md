# Privacy Policy for EvoX
Last Updated: May 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how EvoX ("the Bot") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use it on Discord.

## 1. Information We Collect
To function properly, the Bot collects and stores the following data:
* **Discord User IDs:** To track your XP, levels, and user-specific settings.
* **Discord Guild (Server) IDs:** To save server-specific configurations (like leveling channels, custom messages, and role rewards).
* **Message Activity:** The Bot tracks when you send messages to award XP, but it **does not** store the actual content of your messages.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
The collected data is used strictly for the following purposes:
* To provide the Bot's core features (Leveling system, Rank Cards, Leaderboards).
* To manage server-specific settings set by administrators.

## 3. Data Security
We take the security of your data seriously. We implement appropriate technical measures, including restricted database access, to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure
We value your privacy. Your data is never sold, rented, or shared with third parties. Data is only accessible to the Bot's developer for maintenance and debugging purposes.

## 5. Data Retention and Deletion
Your data is kept as long as the Bot is in your server.
* If the Bot is kicked or removed from a server, all server configurations are kept temporarily but can be purged upon request.
* If you want your personal leveling data deleted, you or a Server Administrator can use the server reset commands, or contact the developer directly.

## 6. Children's Privacy
The Bot is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from anyone under the age of 13.

## 7. Changes to this Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We encourage users to check this page periodically for any changes. Your continued use of the Bot after updates means you accept the revised policy.

## 8. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or want your data removed, please contact the developer via Discord at **ribrotop** or join our support server: [Support Server Link](https://discord.gg/rXEDhF3a2t).
